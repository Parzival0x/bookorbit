## 2026-06-14 - ARIA labels for Search Clear Buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only clear buttons in search inputs are common accessibility traps. Without an ARIA label, screen reader users only hear 'button' without context of what it clears.
**Action:** Always verify that 'X' or clear icons wrapped in buttons have explicit aria-labels describing their specific function (e.g., 'Clear search' rather than just 'Clear').
